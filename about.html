About Me

Hi there! I’m Matea — a physiotherapist from Croatia who took an unexpected turn into the world of computer science. I’m now a second-year Computer Science student at TU Graz, swapping anatomy textbooks for code editors. I feel especially comfortable working in C and C++, where I can really dive into the logic and structure of programming. What I love most is how CS challenges me to think critically and solve problems in creative ways — it’s a different kind of anatomy, just made of logic instead of muscles. What once felt like a distant dream to little Matea is now slowly becoming real — one line of code at a time.
